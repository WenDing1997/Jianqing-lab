Our Lab

We have broad interests in biological invasions, global change biology, biotic interactions and pest managements. We focus on the ecological and evolutionary interactions of plants, insects and AMF fungi. We have been working on the following aspects:
        Biological invasions, pest management, and global changes;
        Co-evolved interactions of invasive plants, AMF fungi and herbivores;
        Above and belowground interactions in natural and agricultural settings;
        Biological control of invasive plants: agent selection; efficacy and non-target effects.
